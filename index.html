About Us

Computer Plaza is an Education centre for Classroom computer Coaching , Computer Professional courses, Abacus and Language Learning courses headed by KUNAL KANTI DAS (the 25 years experienced teacher on different computer programming courses and developer of many business softwares) and PAPIYA DAS (the 15 years teaching experience).
